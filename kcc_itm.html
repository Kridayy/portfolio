About Us

A Premier Technical Institution in Greater Noida

KCC Institute of Technology & Management is affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and approved by AICTE. Situated in Knowledge Park III, Greater Noida, we have been a centre of academic excellence since our founding.

🏛️

AICTE Approved & AKTU Affiliated

All programs are fully recognized by AICTE and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow.

Admissions 2025–26

How to Apply

Admission to B.Tech programs is through JEE Main / UPSEE counselling. MBA/MCA admissions through CMAT/UPCMAT.

01

Fill Application Form

Complete the online application form at our website with your personal and academic details.

02

Appear for Counselling

Qualify JEE Main / UPSEE and participate in AKTU counselling for B.Tech seat allotment.

03

Document Verification

Submit mark sheets, certificates, category certificate (if applicable), and identity proof.

04

Fee Payment & Enrolment

Pay the tuition fee, collect your college ID, and begin your journey at KCC ITM.
